ABOUT IMUKA ACCESS

Imuka Access is a technology driven social enterprise that facilitates direct access to financing and business development support to entrepreneurs in Uganda. We believe in the power of entrepreneurship as a driver of economic growth, employment and poverty reduction. The platform conveniently connects entrepreneurs to business growth opportunities, technical and financial support. As Imuka Access we aim to build the resilience of early stage businesses hence propelling them towards sustainable growth. We envision a thriving and purposeful local enterprise ecosystem, able to foster employment, equity and wealth in Uganda.
